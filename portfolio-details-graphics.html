Comprehensive Design Skillset & Independent Creative Work

As a self-taught graphic designer, I have developed a robust and multifaceted skillset through the use of industry-standard tools like Adobe Photoshop, CorelDRAW, Adobe Illustrator, and InPage. My journey in design has been entirely independent, powered by curiosity and practice—without seeking help from tutorials or external guidance. This not only reflects my commitment to creative learning but also my ability to solve design challenges on my own.

In Adobe Photoshop, I actively utilize essential tools such as the Selection Tool, Crop Tool, and Layer Masking. I’m also comfortable using Adjustment Layers, Blend Modes, and Smart Objects to enhance image quality and preserve design flexibility.

In CorelDRAW, I regularly use the Shape Tool, Contour Tool, and Transparency Tool to apply depth and visual impact. My CorelDRAW work includes designing logos, flyers, and digital cards with strong attention to visual balance and clarity.

Using Adobe Illustrator, I craft high-resolution graphics with the Pen Tool, Artboard Management, and advanced Typography Settings.

I also work with InPage for Urdu calligraphy and layout design, applying custom font styles, spacing, and alignment techniques.
